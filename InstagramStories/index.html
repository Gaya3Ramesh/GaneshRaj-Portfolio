Instagram Stories

Design for mobile

Overview

Instagram stories empowers our users to seamlessly share their Instagram stories directly from within our platform, offering them access to the full spectrum of features available on native Instagram. This means that they can enjoy all the creative tools and options, such as stickers, filters, and text, to enhance and personalize their stories, just as they would on the Instagram app itself.

My Role

Designing the feature end-to-end, working closely with engineers to craft video experiences in detail.

The Team

Solo designer, with guidance from a Lead designer, 1 Product Manager, 7+ engineers.

Timeline

October 2022 - November 2022 Rolled out in January 2023.

Upload, Add some magic then share.

Our platform allows users to seamlessly upload up to 10 different media files, whether they are images or videos, Provided under IG guidelines. In doing so, we offer an array of features and functionalities that closely mirror the capabilities available on Instagram. This ensures that users can enjoy a familiar and comprehensive experience while using our platform.

Increased User Activity:

With the inclusion of Instagram Stories, our users experienced a significant uptick in platform activity. The feature encouraged users to share more frequently, resulting in a 30% increase in daily story posts compared to the pre-implementation period.

Feature Adoption:

Users rapidly embraced the new feature, with over 80% of active users incorporating Instagram Stories into their regular activities within the first month. This high adoption rate indicates that our users valued and appreciated this addition to our platform.

Enhanced User Engagement

The integration of Instagram Stories not only attracted more users but also improved user engagement. The utilization of creative tools, such as stickers, filters, and text, saw a 25% increase in interaction rates within stories, as users were able to personalize and enhance their content easily.

Feature Completeness

Our users lauded the full spectrum of features available within Instagram Stories, comparing them favorably to the native Instagram app. The availability of these creative tools provided users with a seamless and holistic experience, resulting in an 85% user satisfaction rate regarding the completeness of the feature.
