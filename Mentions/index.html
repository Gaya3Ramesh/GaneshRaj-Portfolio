Tag people, Business, pages, and more in your posts.

Mentions allows users to tag peoples, companies, and pages to tag in a single post in various social media platforms.

API Limitations

Instagram's API does not allow us to tag people or pages by simply searching for them. Instead, we need to enter the exact username of the person or page that we want to tag.

Check, delete and add

Once you've listed the users, you have the flexibility to review, manage, remove, or include additional users as needed before finalising and publishing the content. This allows you to carefully curate and tailor your user list to ensure it aligns perfectly with your intended purpose.
