In App Video Editor

Design for mobile

Overview

Zoho Social's built-in video editor provides a convenient and efficient way to create and edit videos for social media. Users can easily trim the Video. additonally They can also change the video thumbnail directly within the app.

My Role

Designing the feature end-to-end, working closely with engineers to craft video experiences in detail.

The Team

1 Product designer (Myself), 1 Product Manager, 7+ engineers.

Timeline

October 2022 - November 2022 Rolled out in January 2023.

Select Pinterest, Choose board.

In our channel selection flow, the sheet serves two primary functions: channel selection and channel pinning. With the integration of Pinterest boards, users can now seamlessly choose a Pinterest channel and create a board within the same user-friendly flow, making the process simpler and more efficient.

Choosing a custom thumbnail

Because all short video platforms support choosing a custom thumbnail for their short videos (e.g., Reels and Shorts), it has become one of the most used features on our platform. We have also observed that the thumbnail in a video plays a very important role in the reach of that particular video.

RESULT

Since the launch of Zoho Social's video editor, we have seen a significant increase in the number of video uploads to our platform. We have also seen a decrease in the number of support tickets related to video uploading, suggesting that users are finding the video editor easy to use. In addition, we have conducted a survey of our users to learn more about how they are using the video editor and what they think of it. The results of the survey were overwhelmingly positive. Users said that the video editor was easy to use, even for those with no prior video editing experience. They also appreciated the ability to trim and edit their videos directly within the Zoho Social app, without having to switch to a separate video editing app.
